Visiting Designer Lecture:

Prem Krishnamurthy

May 3, 6:30pm, CIT 103

Working between design, curating, editing, and teaching Prem lives in New York and Berlin. He is Founding Principal of design studio Workshops, which crafts identities; his previous firm, Project Projects, was the recipient of the Cooper Hewitt’s 2015 National Design Award for Communication Design, the USA’s highest recognition in the field. From 2012–2017, Prem established and directed the multidisciplinary exhibition space P! in New York City’s Chinatown. His experimental, interactive monograph/memoir/manifesto, P!DF, was published by O-R-G in September 2017.
