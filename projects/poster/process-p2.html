Processbook: Project 2

Positioning

The hardest part for me in this project was figuring out where to place each element to look aesthetic but not too wordy. After my critique in class, I decided to change everything and tried out something bolder. I used "VISITING DESIGNER LECTURE SERIES" as a background with subtle blinking so it wouldn't interfere too much with the rest of the page, saving more space for other things since the one I chose has a lot of text. Then I looked for a way to position all the information (picture attached), a way for them to look consistent but still different.

Animation

My second challenge is decidiing on which and where to adapt animation. After all many try and fail, I decided to go with 5 animation: background blinking, text scrolling, name highlighting, paragraph color gradient and rotating flower to minimize white space.
